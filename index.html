About

Founded by Ali Mirza, a second-year medical student at UConn School of Medicine (Class of 2028), Brask Group operates at the intersection of medicine, technology, and business.

I've built 50+ products across healthcare and technology, published first-author research on machine learning applications in medicine, and bring a global perspective shaped by living across four continents—Kenya, Pakistan, Lebanon, and the United States.

My work focuses on making healthcare feel less like bureaucracy and more like hospitality.

"Everything is negotiable"—we believe healthcare can feel like hospitality, not bureaucracy. We're building the tools and systems to make that possible.
