OUR APPROACH

Brask Group is a holding company. We build and operate profitable businesses, then compound value over time.

Right now we focus on healthcare and adjacent markets, but the model is simple: find good businesses, operate them well, allocate capital intelligently.

Current businesses teach us valuable capabilities:

Marketing & operations (JANUS)

Learn how to scale businesses, acquire customers, and grow revenue efficiently.

Consumer healthcare (River)

Understand what people actually want for their health. River specifically is pursuing a vision of delightful healthcare at scale - premium products, then software, then new care delivery models.

Capital allocation (Public investing)

Build track record and learn how to deploy capital effectively.

Talent networks (Experiments)

Find exceptional people who might want to build with us.

The long-term model: operate profitable businesses, allocate capital well, compound value over time.
