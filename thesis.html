HOW WE OPERATE

We're a venture studio. We build, acquire, and operate businesses that compound value. Each venture teaches us capabilities we apply to the next.

Our focus: healthcare, marketing, and software. Our model: find good businesses, operate them well, allocate capital intelligently. The result: a systematic approach to building profitable companies at scale.

Our portfolio companies build institutional knowledge across four core capabilities:

Marketing & Growth Operations

JANUS and OnCall Ads teach us customer acquisition, performance marketing, and scaling revenue. We've taken clients from zero to seven figures, learning what works at scale.

Product Development & Distribution

AppFlow, Med Atlas, Stepordle, and our other products teach us product-market fit, user acquisition, and retention. We build, launch, and iterate quickly.

Capital Allocation

We're building a public track record in markets. This develops our ability to evaluate businesses, deploy capital effectively, and potentially fund future portfolio companies.

Talent & Network

Experiments like The Bara Society help us identify exceptional operators and potential co-founders. We test ideas quickly and build relationships with people who might want to build with us.

The long-term vision: operate profitable businesses, allocate capital well, compound value over time. Each company makes the next one easier to build.
