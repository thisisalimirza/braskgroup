PEARLS

Rapid notes for rounds. Keep only your best pearls.

Pearls helps medical professionals capture clinical pearls quickly, review them with automatic reminders, and build a searchable knowledge library. Export your pearls anywhere.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Quick Capture: Rapidly capture clinical pearls during rounds or study sessions. Start writing immediately.
  • Automatic Reminders: Get notified to review your captured pearls. Decide what to keep and what to discard.
  • Tag & Organize: Tag pearls by specialty (Cardiology, Pulmonology, Neurology, Emergency, etc.) for easy retrieval.
  • Build Your Library: Keep only your best pearls. Swipe away what you don't need. Build a curated knowledge base.
  • Search & Export: Search your library and export pearls anywhere you need them.

FEATURES

  • Rapid capture interface for quick note-taking
  • Automatic review reminders to help you curate
  • Tag-based organization by medical specialty
  • Swipe-to-keep or discard interface
  • Searchable library of your best clinical pearls
  • Export functionality to share or backup
  • Dark mode interface for comfortable use
