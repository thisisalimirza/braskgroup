Rounds

Master USMLE Step 1 through daily clinical cases.

Flashcards like Anki get too granular—you memorize facts but miss the bigger picture. QBanks like UWorld and AMBOSS are comprehensive but overwhelming and expensive.

Rounds fills the gap. Focused, case-based learning with progressive hints that helps you master the diagnosis—the skill that actually matters on test day.

50+ students on the waitlist from 30+ medical schools across the US, UK, Australia, and Pakistan.

How It Works
  • Daily Cases: A new clinical case every day to build your diagnostic skills
  • Progressive Clues: Unlock clinical clues one by one as you work through each case
  • 400+ Cases: Comprehensive library covering all medical specialties
  • Track Progress: Monitor your streak, win rate, and performance over time
  • Browse by Specialty: Filter cases by category—Cardiology, Endocrinology, Urology, and more
Features
  • Daily case challenges to build consistent study habits
  • Random case mode for varied practice
  • Browse 400+ cases organized by medical specialty
  • Statistics tracking: streaks, win rate, guess distribution
  • Progressive hint system to guide learning
  • Score tracking to measure improvement
